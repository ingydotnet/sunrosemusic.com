Sunga Rose is an accomplished singer, band leader, instrumentalist and teacher based in Seattle, Washington. Raised on a diet of musical theater, 70s folk revival, funk and the Beatles, Sunga has been "genre-confused" since childhood. Unwilling to lock herself into any one style, her current musical projects include:

  • Miss Rose & Her Rhythm Percolators: a Vintage Jazz quartet specializing in the music of the 1920s and early 1930s; also available as a duo or trio
  • The Honeyville Rascals: an acoustic duo performing early blues and jazz, Disney hits, folk-rock covers and original tunes; also available as a trio
  • Laughing Rose Duo: another duo that features Americana, Country, pop and more originals
  • Sunga Rose with piano: a vocal/piano show of songs from Broadway and movie musicals and songs from the Great American Songbook
  • Sunga also performs all of these genres as a solo artist and teaches ukulele to students of all ages.

Available for public shows and private events. For information on how to book any of these groups for your special event, please send an email.
